LADIES BANGLE

eka

Defined by its geometric form and balanced composition, this earring design is developed for contemporary gold jewellery collections. The fan-shaped silhouette features a deep green inlay at the center, framed with precision-set stones along the outer edge to create controlled contrast and visual clarity. Fine gold borders enhance the structure while maintaining a clean, lightweight profile. Engineered for consistency in finish and wearability, the design offers a refined addition suited for modern retail assortments.
